Design Overview

The pyrolysis chamber is designed for efficient thermal decomposition of organic materials at elevated temperatures in the absence of oxygen. This model showcases the key components including the reactor vessel, heating elements, gas collection system, and safety features.

Key Features

  • High-temperature resistant alloy construction
  • Multi-zone heating system
  • Advanced thermal insulation
  • Gas collection and filtration system
  • Automated temperature control
  • Safety pressure release valves
